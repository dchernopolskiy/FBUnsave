# Privacy Policy for FB Marketplace Saved Filter

**Last Updated:** December 2025

## Overview

FB Marketplace Saved Filter is a browser extension that helps you filter sold and pending items from your Facebook Marketplace saved listings. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

This extension:
- Does NOT collect or transmit any personal information
- Does NOT track your browsing activity
- Does NOT access any data outside of Facebook Marketplace saved listings pages
- Does NOT communicate with any external servers

## Data Storage

The extension stores only your filter preferences (whether to hide sold items and pending items) locally in your browser using Chrome's sync storage API. This data:
- Is stored locally on your device
- May sync across your Chrome browsers if you're signed into Chrome (this is a Chrome feature, not controlled by us)
- Is never sent to us or any third party

## Permissions Explained

- **storage**: Used to save your filter preferences locally
- **host_permissions (facebook.com/marketplace)**: Required to run the filtering script on Facebook Marketplace pages only

## Third Parties

This extension does not share any data with third parties because it does not collect any data.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above.

## Contact
